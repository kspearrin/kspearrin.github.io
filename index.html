Introducing FlightStage

FlightStage Is A Cloud-based Scheduling Solution for Flight Schools and Flying Clubs

Contact Us To Get Started!

Easy Scheduling

Allow users to quickly and easily reserve aircraft, instructors, simulators, and other equipment from anywhere with an internet connection.

Mobile Support

FlightStage is developed to support the growing mobile market of smart phones, tablets, and PCs. Your users can schedule on the go from anywhere!

Instant Notifications

Automatically notify users when their reservation has changed due to aircraft maintenance, cancellations, etc.

Aircraft Maintenance

Easily manage your fleet by pulling aircraft offline, cancel or shift existing reservations, and logging squawks for users to see while scheduling.

Cloud-based Hosted Solution

No expensive hardware or software installations. FlightStage is hosted and fully managed on our robust cloud network with Amazon AWS.

Customized Look

Retain your professional image. FlightStage allows you to customize the application to use your company colors, logo, and even your website URL.
